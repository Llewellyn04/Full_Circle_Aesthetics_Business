What are Chemical Peels?

A Chemical Peel is a cosmetic procedure that involves applying a chemical solution to the skin that leads to exfoliation and regeneration. Thereafter a neutralizer is applied to halt the chemical reaction initaited by the acid applied to the skin. Neutralizing agents raises the skin pH effectively stopping the acid's action and allowing the skin to begin its healing process.

Benefits

  • Reduce fine lines and wrinkles
  • Improves the appearance of hyperpigmentation and sun damage
  • Improves the overall appearance of acne and acne scarring
  • Stimulates cell turnover
  • Reduce the appearance of enlarged pores

Procedure

The duration of a chemical peel varies based on the type of peel and the specific skin concern. Superficial peels - Medium peels typically take 15-30 minutes. Whereas Medium peels - Deep peels typically take 1-2 hours.
Post treatment your skin may have the following sensations:

  • Redness & sensitivity
  • Tightness & dryness
  • Itchiness or swelling
